Notes & Assumptions:
· Formula: TP_CHF = (COGS + Packaging) / (1 − Gross Margin GWF)  |  Gross Margin = (TP_CHF − COGS) / TP_CHF
· Freight costs are borne by the Distributor (FCA Lucerne) → not included in TP, but added to Distributor Landed Cost.
· US Import Tariff: Applied to transaction value (TP_USD). Currently ~11.7% for CH goods (MFN ~1.7% + Section 122 10%, as of April 2026).
· MPF (Merchandise Processing Fee): 0.3464% of Customs Value, min. USD 31.67 / max. USD 614.35 per shipment — allocated per unit.
· HMF (Harbor Maintenance Fee): 0.125% of Customs Value, sea freight via US ports only.
· This simulation does not replace formal transfer pricing documentation (OECD Guidelines, IRS §482).  |  gwf-group.com
